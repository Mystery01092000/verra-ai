# Verra audit & assurance

## Workflow
1. **Risk-based scope:** identify accounts/assertions; set thresholds.
2. **Test 100% of transactions** (not just samples) for anomalies: weekend/after-hours postings,
   round numbers, backdating, threshold breaches, unusual users; assign a risk score.
3. **Tie out evidence:** invoice ↔ GL ↔ bank; mark Tied / exception with references.
4. **PBC checklist:** track prepared-by-client items; auto-remind.
5. **Workpaper:** assemble a referenced, exportable, defensible trail of procedures + conclusions.

## Rules
- Auditor judgment is final; Verra surfaces and explains, the human concludes.
- Every conclusion links to evidence; everything lands in the immutable audit log.
- Independence/quality standards (PCAOB/AICPA/FRC/ICAI) are respected per jurisdiction.
