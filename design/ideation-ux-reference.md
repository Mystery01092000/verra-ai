# ideation — UX reference (captured from live screenshots, June 2026)

Pages reviewed: Home/Features, Tax Planning, Security, Pricing.

## Visual language
- **Accent:** indigo/periwinkle `#5566FF`; brand gradient `#8A92FF → #4F46E5` (deep indigo at top).
- **Hero:** dreamy lavender/periwinkle **cloud-sky** photo with a faint grid overlay.
- **Headings:** ultra-heavy grotesque **KMR Waldenburg, weight 900**, tight leading. A rotating word
  in the hero ("meetings", etc.).
- **Body:** Inter / system; text `#111`.
- **Pricing card titles:** a **serif** face (mixed with the grotesque).
- **Buttons:** black `#111` pill ("Start 14-day free trial"); solid periwinkle pricing CTAs;
  white-on-dark on dark hero; black "Ask Ideation" sparkle button in-app.
- **Layout:** floating **left vertical nav** (Features/Security/Tax Planning/Pricing);
  "Powered by altruist" bottom-left; white glassy cards; soft shadows; generous whitespace.

## Product UI (in-app)
- **"Your day, prioritized"** feed: date, "All" dropdown, black "Ask Ideation" sparkle, meeting rows with
  calendar icon + time + status tags ("Happening now" green dot, "High net worth"), "View meeting prep".
- **Tax dashboard:** KPI tiles (Total Federal Tax, Marginal Bracket, Effective Rate), "Income Breakdown"
  white card with a green donut + Source/Amount table; indigo **glass insight cards** on the right.
- **Workflow STEP 1–4:** indigo gradient frames with floating UI (chat input "Run the analysis for
  Deborah Millman" + paperclip/mic + 1040 PDF; "Summary" card).

## Pricing
- 3 tiers: Admin AI **$50/seat/mo**, Admin AI + Tax Planning **$125/seat/mo**, Enterprise **Custom**.
- Pay annually / monthly toggle (periwinkle underline). Checkmark feature lists. Off-white cards.

## Security
- AICPA SOC badge; toggles (Save transcripts, Auto-save follow-up emails, Auto-schedule Notetakers).
- Messaging: zero data retention with third-party AI; encryption; SSO; compliance reviews.

> Verra adapts all of the above for accounting/tax/audit/compliance and three tenant types.
